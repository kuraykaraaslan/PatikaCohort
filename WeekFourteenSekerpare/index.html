Sekerpare Recipe

Sekerpare is a Turkish dessert. It is made from flour, sugar, and butter. It is baked and then soaked in a sugar syrup. It is delicious.

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup of flour
  • 1/2 cup of sugar
  • 1/2 cup of butter
  • 1/2 cup of almonds
  • 1/2 cup of sugar for the syrup
  • 1/2 cup of water for the syrup

Instructions:

  1. Mix the flour, sugar, and butter together in a bowl.
  2. Form the dough into small balls and place them on a baking sheet.
  3. Press an almond into the center of each ball.
  4. Bake at 350 degrees for 15 minutes.
  5. While the cookies are baking, make the syrup by boiling the sugar and water together.
  6. When the cookies are done, pour the syrup over them.
